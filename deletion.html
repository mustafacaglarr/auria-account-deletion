Delete Your Auria Account

App: Auria – Virtual Try-On

You can delete your Auria account and associated data at any time.

Delete inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

This will permanently delete your account, uploaded photos, try-on images, and profile data from our systems.
